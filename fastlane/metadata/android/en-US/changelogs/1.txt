Initial beta release.

- Four EQ modes: Parametric, Graphic, Table, Simple.
- Up to 16 bands, 5 filter types (Bell, Shelf, Pass).
- Multiband compressor (1-6 bands) with soft-knee and GR traces.
- Limiter with waveform meter and LUFS metering.
- Real-time FFT spectrum analyzer via the Visualizer API.
- AutoEQ target-curve fitter.
- APO import/export for desktop Equalizer APO interchange.
- Custom preset library with mini-graph thumbnails.
