Initial beta release.

- Four EQ modes: Parametric, Graphic, Table, Simple.
- Up to 16 bands, five filter types (Bell, Low/High Shelf, Low/High Pass).
- Multiband compressor (1 to 6 bands) with soft-knee and per-band GR traces.
- Limiter with input/output waveform meter and LUFS metering.
- Real-time FFT spectrum analyzer driven by the Visualizer API.
- AutoEQ target-curve fitter.
- APO import/export for preset interchange with desktop Equalizer APO.
- Custom preset library with mini-graph thumbnails.
